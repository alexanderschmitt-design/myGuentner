    Important Remarks / Regulation, Standards
    
              
    
                In "IMPACT", a tool to assess the costs of air-cooled heat surface products running on air-cooled condensers in the unit's lifetime. This tool indicates the total cost of ownership of the heat exchanger, estimated by considering initial purchasing investment and energy costs over the assumed service life. The rating (1 = very good to 5 = insufficient) enables a cost-optimized selection of units and facilitates comparison.
              
    
              
    
                Regulation: EU Regulation 2016/2281 (Ecodesign for medium and large air heating products, cooling products and high temperature process chillers). The energy label is pursuant to the Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) No 2015/1094.
              
    
              
    
                Any IMPACT calculation is based on operating conditions as per EU regulation 2016/2281 (Ecodesign). Data provided serves as a general estimate and may differ during actual system operation. Guntner does not assume liability for deviations between IMPACT results and actual energy consumption/costs during operation, as these depend on system-specific boundary conditions and installation.
              
    
              
    
                In accordance with EU Regulation 2016/2281, the energy efficiency of the air cooler is determined at full load operating point under standardized conditions. The SFPint value (Specific Fan Power internal) indicates the electrical power consumption of the fans related to the air volume flow. The COP value (Coefficient of Performance) indicates the ratio of useful cooling capacity to electrical power consumption.
              
    
              
    
                (1) The useful cooling capacity (Q_usable) is determined in accordance with EN 328 and takes into account the surface reserve of the heat exchanger, the defrost energy and the fan heat. Values at full load operating point.
              
    
              
    
                The forecast of defrost intervals and duration does not replace the optimal defrost setting made by the system installer when commissioning the system (see installation instructions). It must be adapted to the operating conditions and is not our responsibility. During operation, the values may deviate from the forecasts.

Guntner reserves the right to modify specifications without notice. For binding quotations, please contact your local Guntner sales representative.
